The Mission

Measuring Advancement with Facts.

The Green Tape Measure is on a mission to champion the next generation of interior designers. Through data-driven advocacy, we’re expanding career paths and protecting public safety by ensuring the profession is recognized, regulated, and respected.

Founding

Grounded in Teaching.

GTM was initially created in 2025 as a platform to expand the founder’s professional tutoring services. After encountering job security, equal pay, and leadership barriers and hearing from other designers struggling with similar obstacles, she decided GTM could transform into an industry resource for evidence-based advocacy.

HSW in Action

Our Legislative Voice.

We advocate for inclusive and data-supported regulation that elevates the profession and protects the public.

April 2026

Our Legislative Goals

Create diverse licensing tracks, including non-degree options and exams tailored to specific practice areas like residential or commercial design.

Establish independent or equal-representation boards and implement mandatory, non-voluntary licensing for all interior design practitioners.

Secure exclusive use of "Interior Designer" and "Licensed Interior Designer" while restricting "Interior Architect" and mandating "Licensed Architect" for architectural professionals.

Grant designers full permitting privileges, including the authority to sign and seal plans without restrictions, including on square footage, occupancy, construction scope, or hiring and coordination of professions.
