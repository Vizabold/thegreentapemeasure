The Mission

Measuring Advancement with Facts.

The Green Tape Measure is on a mission to champion the next generation of interior designers. Through data-driven advocacy, we’re expanding career paths and protecting public safety by ensuring the profession is recognized, regulated, and respected.

Founding

Grounded in Teaching.

GTM was initially created in 2025 as a platform to expand the founder’s professional tutoring services. After encountering job security, equal pay, and leadership barriers and hearing from other designers struggling with similar obstacles, she decided GTM could transform into an industry resource for evidence-based advocacy.
